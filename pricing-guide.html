Case Study #1: Custom Home Builder

The Challenge

This client needed a way to clearly communicate the value of their unique VR design service. In our discovery conversation, they also expressed a general frustration around market visibility.

Our Strategy

After reviewing customer feedback together, we isolated the primary reason customers chose their company. In one word: trustworthiness. We also spent time clarifying the primary value of the VR service to customers: peace of mind before building.

We then built a strategy that leaned hard into those strengths:

Produced two emotionally compelling long-form videos: A homepage testimonial video to highlight trustworthiness, and an explainer video for their VR page to emphasize both peace of mind and trustworthiness.
Repurposed content from the same shoot into various strategically placed media marketing assets to boost market visibility.

Results (within 3 months of launch)

  • 60% increase in VR-related inquiries
  • 20% boost in website conversions
  • 15% increase in social engagement
  • Prospects frequently mentioned the videos during sales calls, indicating stronger brand recall

Case Study #2:
Oral Surgery & Dental Implant Center

The Challenge

This practice wanted to strengthen their online presence and grow their patient base. They offered premium care but weren’t seeing that translate into new patient inquiries, and website visitors weren’t converting.

Our Strategy

We designed a content plan around two goals:

Build trust through story
Educate clearly on complex procedures

Each quarter, we produce a long-form patient/Dr story video and multiple short-form edits that emphasize the clinic’s strengths. We also include mini educational clips that address FAQs around procedures and recovery.

Results (within the first 6 months)

  • 30% increase in dental implant consultations
  • 2x engagement on educational content
  • 160% increase in conversions from paid traffic
  • Notable SEO improvements on video-optimized service pages

Case Study #3:
Construction & Engineering Industrial Group

The Challenge

This industrial group was facing a shortage of qualified tradespeople, limiting their ability to scale. Competitors were actively courting skilled workers through clearer messaging on culture and opportunities.

Our Strategy

We developed a recruitment media campaign that put their real employees front and center. We highlighted their stories, the quality of their training, and the company’s strong safety culture.

We paired long-form storytelling with short-form assets specifically designed for LinkedIn, trade school outreach, hiring fairs, and other social media.

Results (within the first 10 months)

  • 120% increase in applications overall
  • 40% increase in applications from trade schools
  • Internal referrals increased by 25%
  • HR team reported improved candidate quality and higher interview-to-hire ratio
